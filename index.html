I am an M.S.-to-Ph.D. student in Computer Technology at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU), Xi'an, China, supervised by Prof. Wei Wei. During my master's stage (2024 – 2025), I was supervised by Assoc. Prof. Runping Xi. Before that, I obtained my B.Eng. in Computer Science and Technology from Hunan Normal University in 2024, supervised by Prof. Jianhua Dai.

My research focuses on Multi-Agent Systems, Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs), and Visual Tokenizers.
